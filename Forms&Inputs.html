Forms

Forms are used to collect user input in web applications. They can include various types of input fields such as text boxes, checkboxes, radio buttons, and dropdowns.

Inputs

Inputs are the individual fields within a form where users can enter data. They can be of different types, such as text, password, email, number, etc.
